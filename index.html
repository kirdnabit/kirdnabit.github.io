Shoegaze: An Overview

Shoegaze is a genre known for its introspective themes and escapist lyrics, as well as a desire to create dreamy landscapes perfect for daydreaming and getting away from the mundane.

Important Terms to Know

Term Definition
Delay Time based effect that creates a repeated echo of the original input
Distortion Umbrella term for all effects that adds thickness to a signal's waveform
Drone Sustained or repeated tones used to create a hypnotic or immersive musical atmosphere
Lo-fi Production style that embraces imperfections such as noise and distortion to create a raw or intimate sound
Reverb Effect used to give an input signal a natural, open room feeling
Soundscape The overall sonic environment created by combining layered instruments and effects
Wall of Sound Characteristic music production technique created by densly layering instruments to create an immersive auditory experience
